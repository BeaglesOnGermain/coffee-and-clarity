About Sandra Melvin, CLC

Being truly heard
is deeply meaningful.

Coffee & Clarity was born from a simple belief: people are often carrying more than they show. When someone feels comfortable, unhurried, and genuinely listened to, they often find the clarity they've been searching for.

Sandra brings a warm, compassionate, and intuitive presence to every conversation. Her work blends deep listening, intuitive insight, thoughtful reflection, and practical encouragement.

Like sea air clearing the mind, Coffee & Clarity is meant to offer a moment of spaciousness, perspective, and gentle forward movement — right here in Saint John.

A Note About What This Is

Coffee & Clarity is not therapy and Sandra Melvin is not a therapist. These sessions are supportive, conversation-based intuitive guidance designed to offer a compassionate, grounded space for reflection. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or require clinical care, Sandra encourages you to connect with an appropriate healthcare professional.
